# ADV-KIT Research — Framework Boundary Verdicts

Assumptions:
- Judge component nature, not current app usage.
- "KEEP-IN-CORE" can mean a curated subpath such as `@godxjp/ui/query`, not necessarily the root barrel.
- Hardcoded locale/country/status/business constants are app data even when the rendered control is reusable.
- Source is mid-refactor; verdicts target the concept and future package boundary.

- MUI component catalog lists App Bar, Card, Badge, Skeleton, Stack, Grid, Container, Data Grid, Date and Time Pickers, Tree View: https://mui.com/components/
- MUI X DatePicker docs: https://mui.com/x/react-date-pickers/date-picker/
